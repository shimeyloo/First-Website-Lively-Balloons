I used to work at a balloon store and was a regular ordering balloons for my friends and family. I eventually became an employee for the place that I always bought balloons from! I later realized the joy that balloons could bring to people.

After working there for a couple years, I thought that It was time for me to move onto something even greater. I moved to Santa Monica, but the balloon store that I was working with was not what I had expected and did not have my interests in mind. That moment was short lived.

I have this great passion working with balloons and visions that balloons can become. I want to show people that there is more to balloons than meets the eye. I decided to move back to the central valley and decided that I would open up my own balloon shop! I now have my store where I can show you all how lively balloons can be! I look forward to working with everyone and helping you create unforgettable memories at any and all of your events!
